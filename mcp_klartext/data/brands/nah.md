# Nah?

## Voice
- **Perspective:** "we" (the Nah? team/community)
- **Register:** Direct, technical but accessible. Open-source ethos.
- **Tone:** Opinionated but transparent. "Here's our trade-off, here's why." Privacy-first values.
- **Signature moves:** Problem statement → honest caveat → architectural decision → invitation

## Language
- **Primary:** German (community is DACH-focused)
- **Secondary:** English (techfeed/development content)
- **Hashtags:** DE hashtags (#ondernemen, #datenschutz, #gemeinschaft) + EN technical tags

## Visual
- **Aesthetic:** Lo-fi, intentional imperfection. Anti-surveillance, anti-attention-economy.
- **Palette:** Muted earth tones, organic colors. No neon, no gradients.
- **Typography:** Rounded, approachable. Feels handmade.
- **Photography:** Real people, local places. No stock. Community first.
- **Never:** Glossy, corporate, anything that looks like Big Tech
