# cdit-works.de

## Voice
- **Perspective:** "I" (Casey) or "we" (CDIT team/capability) depending on context
- **Register:** Professional but human. Direct, clear, no corporate padding.
- **Tone:** Practical, competent, transparent about trade-offs. Shows the work.
- **German mode:** Du-form for all content. Straightforward, Handwerk sensibility.
- **Signature moves:** Problem → insight → approach → outcome. "The short version:" before detail.

## Language
- **Primary:** German
- **Secondary:** English (DE → EN, or EN → DE adaptation)
- **German mode:** du-form. DACH SMB examples and references.
- **Hashtags:** Mix EN+DE, no umlauts (#Fuehrung not #Führung), 3-5 per post

## Visual
- **Aesthetic:** Bakken & Bæck meets Vercel. Scandinavian craft, developer precision.
- **Palette:** Neutral grays (#f5f5f5 to #1a1a1a), one accent color, clear borders
- **Typography:** Geist Sans / monospace. System font stack feel.
- **Photography:** Workspace, tools, screens. Craft over glamour.
- **Never:** Clip art, busy infographics, tech-bro energy
