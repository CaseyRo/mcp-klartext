# StoryKeep

## Voice
- **Perspective:** "we" (CDIT as partner) in client comms. Third-person for public content.
- **Register:** Professional, warm, curatorial. Museum world vocabulary.
- **Tone:** Considered, respectful of the art/heritage. Technical detail serves the story.

## Language
- **Primary:** English (client is US/NL-based)
- **Secondary:** Dutch (casual correspondence with Piet, "Kees" register)

## Visual
- **Aesthetic:** Pantone-grounded, museum-grade. White space as design element.
- **Palette:** Pulled from exhibition content. Neutral frames, rich content.
- **Typography:** Elegant serif for exhibition titles. Clean sans for navigation.
- **Photography:** High-resolution exhibition shots, archival materials, gallery interiors.
- **Never:** Playful, casual, social-media-first aesthetic
